Bachelor of Computer Applications

BCA (Bachelor of Computer Applications) is an undergraduate academic degree in the field of computer science. It typically spans three years but in 2024 it four years and covers a wide range of computer science and application-related subjects.Here are the key details about the BCA course:

  1. Eligibility:
    • Students with a Computer Science background are often drawn to BCA.
    • Minimum 50% marks in 10+2 (class 12) in any relevant stream with English as a mandatory subject.
    • There is a relaxation of 5% for reserved category candidates (ST, SC, OBC, etc.).
  2. Admission Process:
    • BCA admission occurs through entrance exams or merit-based selection.
    • Entrance exams include CUET, SET, IPU CET, MET, among others.
    • Direct admission is based on class 12 marks.
  3. Course Subjects:
    • Data Structures
    • Programming languages like C, paython and Java
    • management
    • computer architechture
  4. Career Opportunities:    BCA graduates can work as:
    • Software Developers
    • System Analysts
    • Web Designers
    • And more!
