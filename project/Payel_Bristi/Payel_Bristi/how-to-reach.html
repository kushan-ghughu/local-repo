Travel Methods

By Air

The nearest airport is Netaji Subhas Chandra Bose International Airport in Kolkata, which is approximately 150 km away. From the airport, you can hire a taxi or take a bus to Bishnupur.

By Train

Bishnupur has its own railway station which is well connected to major cities like Kolkata, Howrah, and others. Trains are a convenient and comfortable way to reach Bishnupur.

By Road

Bishnupur is well connected by road. Regular bus services are available from Kolkata and other nearby cities. You can also drive down to Bishnupur from Kolkata via NH19 and NH14.
